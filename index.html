How to Play

  • Move Paddle:
    Drag with two fingers, alternatively with one finger for smaller screens.
  • Rotate Paddle:
    Use two fingers to twist.
  • Nudge:
    Double-tap to give the ball a push!
  • Goal:
    Keep the ball in play as long as possible!
  • Rules:
    The higher the difficulty, the higher the ball speed but also the score scaling!
